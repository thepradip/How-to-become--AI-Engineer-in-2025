Prompt enhancement checklist

TaskState the exact job, audience, and definition of done.
ContextAdd files, data, examples, constraints, and source rules.
FormatAsk for JSON, Markdown, code patch, table, or final answer style.
CritiqueRequest risks, missing tests, assumptions, and improvement ideas.
